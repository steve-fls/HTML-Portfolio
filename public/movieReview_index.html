The Best Movies According to Steve

Top Three Movies

Gangs of New York

Available on HBO-Max

This drama based on the New York ganks from the 1800s is has
an elite actor lineup who all bring their best to the film.
Great story lines with deep characters who realize themselves as the the
story unfolds. Bill, the Butcher is the main antoginist of the movie who is
one of my favorites in cinema being both each to hate and love at the same time.

The Princess Bride

Available on Disney+

I am a big fan of the old classic love comedy. Its characters are fun and humor is
classy while simple. Its got a few great fight sequences which really just emphasis
the true talent of the actors themselves.

Bullet-Train

Available on Netflix

This action comedy has a great cast with some extrodinary choices for certain roles.
The action is high impact while always being kept lowkey since everything is
happending on a plublic train. Great humor and great action sequences.
